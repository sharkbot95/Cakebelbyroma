Our Story

Where Passion
Meets Perfection

"Every cake is a canvas. Every celebration, a story worth telling."

Cakebel by Roma was born from a simple belief — every celebration deserves a cake as unique and beautiful as the person being honoured. What began as Roma's personal passion for baking has grown into Bengaluru's most trusted custom cake studio, nestled in the heart of Jigani.

With an artist's eye for detail and a baker's reverence for flavour, Roma handcrafts each cake from the finest, freshest ingredients. No two cakes are alike. Each is a conversation — a story told in chocolate, sugar flowers, and buttercream — made exclusively for you.
